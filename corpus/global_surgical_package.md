# The Global Surgical Package

> Illustrative reference text for demonstration. Not authoritative or current.

## What is included

The global surgical package bundles the services normally furnished by a surgeon
before, during, and after a procedure into a single payment. Depending on the
procedure, the global period may be 0, 10, or 90 days. Services included in the
package are not separately payable during the global period.

## Typically included

The package generally includes the operation itself, local or topical anesthesia
provided by the surgeon, and routine postoperative care such as uncomplicated
follow-up visits related to recovery from the procedure. For procedures with a
90-day global period, one related preoperative evaluation and management visit on
the day before or day of surgery is also included.

## Typically not included

Services unrelated to the diagnosis for which the procedure was performed,
treatment of the underlying condition that is not part of normal recovery, a
return to the operating room for a related problem, and care for complications
that require a separate trip to the operating room may be separately reportable
with the appropriate modifier.

## Relevant modifiers

Modifier 24 identifies an unrelated E/M service during a postoperative period.
Modifier 79 identifies an unrelated procedure during the postoperative period.
Modifier 78 identifies a return to the operating room for a related procedure
during the postoperative period. These modifiers signal that the service falls
outside the routine global package.
